About the Clinic

Our school clinic is dedicated to promoting the health and wellness of every student. We provide a safe, caring environment where students can receive immediate medical attention, wellness monitoring, and guidance on healthy living.

Our team of trained health professionals works closely with teachers and parents to ensure that every student's health needs are met efficiently and with the utmost confidentiality.

Through our Medical Information Management System (MIMS), students can now request appointments digitally, and clinic staff can manage health records more effectively than ever before.

Clinic Services

💊

First Aid & Medication

Immediate care for minor injuries, wounds, headaches, fever, and other acute concerns.

📏

Health Monitoring

Regular height, weight, and BMI assessments to track student wellness and growth.

🩺

Medical Consultation

One-on-one consultation with the school nurse for health concerns and referrals.

🦷

Dental Referral

Assessment and referral for dental-related issues requiring further attention.

🧠

Mental Health Support

Guidance and referrals for students experiencing stress, anxiety, or emotional difficulties.

📋

Medical Records

Maintenance of student health records including history, exposures, and clinic visits.
